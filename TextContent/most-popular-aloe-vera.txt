<span class="startLetter">Aloe vera</span><br>
                    Aloe vera is a variety of
                    houseplant that is most known
                    for its medical benefits. It has
                    been grown for many years
                    and cultivated for its medicinal
                    purposes. You can use the
                    healthy compounds of the aloe
                    vera plant to ease scrapes and
                    burns, so it is a great plant to
                    have around the house.
